## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
PMO CASCADE Sovereign Engine is a professional UAE real estate feasibility platform that transforms 5 simple inputs into comprehensive engineering analysis across 12 domains.

FEATURES:
• Visual Design Catalog — Choose architectural style, exterior/interior finishes, landscape, and facilities from photo catalogs
• Automatic Quality Inference — System determines quality level from your selections
• Cascade Engine — Processes input through zoning, cost, structural, fire safety, and area calculations
• 12 Domain Chain — Project & Plot, Location & GIS, Stakeholders, Zoning, Validation, Design, Unit Mix, Cost, Schedule, Approvals, Geotechnical, Sustainability
• 3D Structural Model — Interactive Three.js viewer with columns, core, shear walls
• 2D Floor Plan — Auto-generated structural plan with grid lines and dimensions
• Bill of Quantities — Priced BOQ with UAE market rates (AED)
• Excel Export — Professional XLSX with Summary, Zoning, Cost Breakdown, Schedule
• A3 Poster — Print-ready poster with all key metrics
• Project Save/Load — Save projects to database, retrieve anytime
• N8n Automation — Webhook endpoints for workflow automation

TARGET AUDIENCE:
• Real estate developers in UAE
• Project managers and feasibility analysts
• Architecture and engineering consultants
• Investment analysts evaluating UAE properties

COVERAGE:
• 7 UAE Emirates: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, RAK, Ajman, UAQ, Fujairah
• Qatar: Doha, Lusail, Al Wakrah
• 20 building types: Residential, Commercial, Mixed-Use, Hotel, Healthcare, Education
• 4 quality levels: Basic, Standard, Premium, Luxury

STANDARDS:
• ISO 9001, 14001, 45001
• Dubai Building Code
• Estidama Pearl Rating
• Qatar Construction Specifications (QCS)
• PMI-PMBOK, FIDIC, CESMM4
